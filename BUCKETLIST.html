My Bucket List

1. Travel to all 7 continents

Ever since I was a child, I've been fascinated by different cultures and places around the world. I've already been to Europe and Asia, but I still have four more continents to explore: Africa, South America, Australia, and Antarctica. Some specific places I'd love to visit include:

  • The pyramids of Egypt
  • The beaches of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
  • The Great Barrier Reef in Australia
  • The wildlife of the Serengeti in Tanzania
  • The penguins of Antarctica

2. Learn a new language fluently

I've always been fascinated by languages and the way they can connect people from different parts of the world. I've studied several languages in school, but I've never become truly fluent in any of them. I'd love to pick one language and become so comfortable with it that I could have a conversation with a native speaker without stumbling or relying on a dictionary. Some languages I'm considering include:

  • Spanish
  • Mandarin Chinese
  • Arabic
  • French
  • Japanese

3. Write and publish a book

Writing has always been a passion of mine, and I've dreamed of seeing my words in print since I was a teenager. I have a few different ideas for books I'd like to write, including a memoir about my travels, a novel set in a different time period, and a non-fiction book about language and culture. I know that writing a book is a huge undertaking, but I'm committed to making it happen someday.

If you have any suggestions or tips for achieving these goals, or if you have your own bucket list items to share, please feel free to contact me.
